Portal (2007)

"Portal" and "Portal 2" are the best puzzle games ever. Loved both of them.

Mass Effect Trilogy (2012)

I haven't played the remastered Legendary Trilogy Edition and did not finish "Mass Effect: Andromeda" yet, but the original trilogy is another series I adore, despite the ending of the third installment being disappointing before the patch it received.

My Rating for the Mass Effect Trilogy is: 9/10.
