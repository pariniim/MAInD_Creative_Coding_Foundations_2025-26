Destroy the bricks!

User your arrow keys to move the green paddle on the bottom of the playground. User the left arrow key to move it to the left; use the right arrow key to move the paddle to the right.

Try to hit the blue bricks by letting the ball bounce around the playground, from one wall to the other. Destroy all bricks to win the game.

If the ball hits the floow (bottom wall), you will lose one life. Once you have lost all lives, the game ends.
